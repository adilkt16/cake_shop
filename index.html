FAQ

Hear Us Out
  • Do you offer custom cake designs?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom-designed cakes. Our talented team will work closely with you to bring your vision to life and create a unique and personalized cake for your special occasion.

  • Do you deliver cakes?

Yes, we offer cake delivery within a specified radius from our bakery. Delivery charges may apply, depending on the distance. We take extra care to ensure that your cake arrives in perfect condition and on time for your event.

  • What sizes of cakes do you offer?

We offer cakes in various sizes to accommodate different party sizes and serving needs. Our sizes range from personal-sized mini cakes to larger sheet cakes that can serve a crowd. Let us know your requirements, and we will guide you in selecting the appropriate size.
